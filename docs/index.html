Building for the 22nd Century

Where 21st-Century Technology Meets Common-Sense American Leadership

The Mission

We are told that to be a "patriot" is to look backward—to return to the factories, the fields, and the isolation of the 19th century. We are told that "freedom" means a government that is either a weapon or a ghost

I disagree.

I am a web developer, a student of history, and a North Carolinian. I see a world where "the more things change, the more they stay the same:" Just as the Industrial Revolution once demanded a new way of thinking, the Digitial and Automation Revolutions of today demand that we stop waving the white flag of nostaligia.

This is a site for the Builders who are tired of those tearing the world down.

Why This Site?

The future is open-source. I believe in transparency, technical literacy, and the idea that the best solutions come from logic and data, not just rhetoric and "doublespeak".

Whether you are a Blue Dog Democrat looking for fiscal sanity, or a Progressive looking for a bold vision of the future, you have a seat at this table.

Let's stop arguing about the 19th century and start building for the 22nd.
